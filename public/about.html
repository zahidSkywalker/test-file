Our Story

Founded in 2020, TrendyMart emerged from a simple vision: to make premium electronics accessible to every household in Bangladesh. What started as a small local shop in Dhaka has now grown into the country's most trusted online electronics marketplace.

Our journey began with a team of passionate tech enthusiasts who believed that quality electronics shouldn't be a luxury. We started by offering a carefully curated selection of smartphones, laptops, and accessories, focusing on authentic products and exceptional customer service.

Today, TrendyMart serves customers across all 64 districts of Bangladesh, with a network of over 50 authorized service centers and partnerships with leading global brands including Apple, Samsung, Sony, LG, and many more.

Our Journey

From a small local shop to Bangladesh's leading electronics marketplace - here's our story of growth and success.

2020 - The Beginning

Founded in Dhaka

TrendyMart was established with a vision to make premium electronics accessible to everyone in Bangladesh. Started as a small local shop with just 5 employees.

2021 - Digital Expansion

Launched Online Platform

Launched our e-commerce platform, expanding our reach beyond Dhaka. Partnered with major brands and established our first service center network.

2022 - Market Leadership

Became Market Leader

Achieved market leadership position with presence in all major cities. Expanded service network to 25 centers and reached 100,000+ customers.

2023 - Innovation & Growth

Technology Innovation

Introduced AI-powered recommendations, mobile app, and advanced logistics. Expanded to 50+ service centers and served 500,000+ customers.

2024 - Future Forward

Leading the Future

Continuing to innovate with cutting-edge technology, expanding product categories, and maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction and product authenticity.
